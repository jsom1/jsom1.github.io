Hello, World!

Welcome to my personal page. It's early 2020, and I decided to start this website to store everything I do related to my preparation for the OSCP. This includes :

  • CTFs from VulnHub & Hack The Box
  • Book summaries
  • Other useful resources

But why ?

When I decided to learn more about cybersecurity, I didn't really know where to start. I had a very basic knowledge of networking, and I didn't know much about hacking...
I started with courses on Coursera, but I didn't get what I was expecting: it was very theoretical and I was looking for hands-on experience.

After some research, I found Vulnhub and Hack The Box. I also looked for serious certifications and found those of Offensive Security (the developers of Kali Linux). Finally, I bought some good books and found free courses on Cybrary...

I had everything I needed to start: a goal, hands-on exercises, and a ton of things to learn from the books and courses.
I started taking notes about the books and documented what I was doing in the boxes. I was doing it in Google drive, when I suddenly thought about regrouping everything in one place that would serve one purpose: telling my journey. Bonus, I would get introduced to HTML and be able to share my experience with anyone that would be interested.
This is what this site is all about.

Feel free to visit !
