Why am I doing this

Until recently, I had been thinking about shifting my career path towards cybersecurity for a while.
When I decided to learn more about the topic, I didn't really know where to start. I had very basic knowledge of how networks work, but I didn't know much about hacking.
I started with courses on Coursera, but I didn't get what I was expecting. It was very theoretical and I needed hands-on experience. Like many others, I learn best by doing...

After some research, I found Vulnhub and Hack The Box. I also looked for serious certifications that could help me finding a job someday and found those of Offensive Security. Finally, I bought some good books and found freee courses on Cybrary...
I had everything I needed to start : a goal, hands-on exercises, and a ton of things to learn from the books and courses.
I started taking notes about the books and documented what I was doing in the boxes. I was doing it in Google drive, when I suddenly thought about regrouping everything in one place that would serve one purpose : telling my journey. Bonus, I would get introduced to HTML and be able to share my experience with anyone that would be interested.
